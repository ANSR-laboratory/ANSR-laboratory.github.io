# [Applied Neuroimaging Statistics Research Laboratory](https://github.com/ANSR-laboratory/ANSR-laboratory.github.io)

**About**
This repository hosts the official website of the Applied Neuroimaging Statistics Research (ANSR) Laboratory, based at the McLean Imaging Center, McLean Hospital, within the Department of Psychiatry at Harvard Medical School. The website provides information about our research, team members, publications, job openings, and ways to get in touch.

**Website Overview**
The website is structured into the following sections:
- About: Overview of the ANSR Lab, including our mission and research focus.
- Members: Profiles of lab members, including faculty, research staff, postdocs, and trainees.
- Research: Description of our research areas, projects, and methodologies.
- Publications: A collection of our latest published and preprint research articles.
- Job Openings: Information on available research positions and opportunities to join the lab.
- Contact: How to reach us for collaborations, inquiries, or general information.

**Technology & Framework**
The website is built using [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/), a fast and flexible static site generator. It is styled with the [Hugo Research Group Theme](https://github.com/HugoBlox/theme-research-group), which is specifically designed for academic and research labs.
